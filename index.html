Our Story

Crafted for the
Refined Soul

AURUM Parfums was born from a belief that world-class fragrances should be accessible to every Bangladeshi who appreciates the finer things in life. We source the finest oud from the Middle East, rose from Bulgaria, and blend them in our Dhaka atelier.

Each bottle is a celebration of heritage, craftsmanship, and modern elegance — made to be worn, gifted, and remembered.
